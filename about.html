A sweet Cape Cod stop

Somerset Creamery is a beloved local ice cream shop in Cataumet on Cape Cod, known for serving fresh, homemade ice cream in a welcoming, family-friendly environment. We’re proud to be part of the community — a place where locals bring friends, tourists become regulars, and kids plan their next scoop before the first one melts.

From classic flavors to seasonal favorites, we focus on simple ingredients, generous portions, and that summer-on-Cape-Cod feeling — salty air, sunshine, and a cone in hand.
